Refund Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2024

Refund Eligibility: As we do not facilitate the sale of products, refunds are subject to the policies of the individual sellers. Each seller may have their own refund policy, and it is recommended to review this information on the product listing or contact the seller directly for details. Refund Process: If a refund is applicable based on the seller's policies, the refund process will be handled by the seller. Please contact the seller directly to initiate any refund requests. Contacting Sellers: For any refund-related inquiries or concerns, please reach out to the seller directly. Contact information for the seller is typically available on the product listing or can be requested through our app.
